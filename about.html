Our Belief

We are a conservative and evangelical congregation in The Anglican Diocese of All Nations. We believe in Jesus Christ as Lord, Savior and the only way to eternal life. We believe the Bible as the inspired word of God and we seek transformation from it through the help of the Holy Spirit. We worship God in spirit and truth in the traditional Anglican liturgy with some flexibilities.
