Men’s Christian Fellowship (MCF)

Men’s Christian Fellowship is a group for all men in Holy Trinity Anglican Church. The aim of MCF is to support one another in spiritual growth and keeping (to honor) all marriage vows. To give guidance on how to raise a godly family. To encourage one another regularly attend church services meetings. Most importantly, to win others (souls) for Christ through a life of love and service.

Women’s Ministry

Women’s ministry is an organization for fellowship amongst the women of Holy Trinity Anglican Church. The purpose of Women’s Ministry is to be especially concerned with all that strengthens and preserves family life. Membership is open to all women in the church. Members are united in prayer and service to show forth the glory of God and support the work of the church.

Youth & Young Adults

The Youth and Young Adult Ministry is group where youths are challenged to live for Christ and be focused with academic pursuit. Also, they learn how to be involved in church ministry as well as connect with other Christian youths. Young Adults challenge one another to pursue their life goals and build a career. Our Young Adults also learn tips for healthy relationships and marriage preparations. They are involved in social and community services.

Children’s Ministry

Welcome to our Children’s Ministry, where we nurture young hearts to grow in faith and love for Christ! Our mission is to provide a safe, fun, and Christ-centered environment where children can explore their relationship with God, build friendships, and develop strong Christian values. We offer engaging programs such as Sunday School, Vacation Bible School, and creative activities designed to teach Bible lessons in an exciting and interactive way. Through worship, crafts, storytelling, and outreach projects, we inspire children to live out their faith and make a difference in the world.
