Men’s Christian Fellowship (MCF)

The Men’s Christian Fellowship (MCF) is a group for all men in Holy Trinity Anglican Church. Its mission is to uphold one another in spiritual growth and uphold the sanctity of marriage. MCF provides guidance to Men on raising godly families, uplifting one another and encourages regular participation in church services and activities. Above all, the fellowship strives to lead others to Christ through lives marked by love, service, discipline and commitment to sharing the Gospel.

Women’s Ministry

Women’s Ministry is fellowship for women of Holy Trinity Anglican Church. Its purpose is to focus on strengthening and preserving family life through faith and care for one another. Membership is open to all women in the church. Members are united in prayer and service to show forth the glory of God and actively support the work of God in the church.

Youth & Young Adults

The Youth and Young Adult Ministry provides a space for young people to be challenged in their faith and committed to living for Christ while excelling academically. The ministry encourages active involvement in church life and fosters connections with other Christian youth. Young Adults are empowered to pursue their personal and professional goals, while also learning valuable insights on building healthy relationships and preparing for marriage. Additionally, they are engaged in social outreach and community service, embodying Christ’s love in action.

Children’s Ministry

Welcome to our Children’s Ministry, where we nurture young hearts to grow in faith and love for Christ! Our mission is to provide a safe, fun, and Christ-centered environment where children can explore their relationship with God, build friendships, and develop strong Christian values. We offer engaging programs such as Sunday School, Vacation Bible School, and creative activities designed to teach Bible lessons in an exciting and interactive way. Through worship, crafts, storytelling, and outreach projects, we inspire children to live out their faith and make a difference in the world.

Men’s Christian Fellowship (MCF)

Men’s Christian Fellowship is a group for all men in Holy Trinity Anglican Church. The aim of MCF is to support one another in spiritual growth and keeping (to honor) all marriage vows. To give guidance on how to raise a godly family. To encourage one another regularly attend church services meetings. Most importantly, to win others (souls) for Christ through a life of love and service.
